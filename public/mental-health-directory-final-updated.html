📊 Recovery Tools & Progress Tracking

DIALOG+ Wellbeing Matrix: An evidence-based tool that helps measure progress across 11 life domains including mental health, physical health, accommodation, work, finances, and relationships. Used in NHS services to track recovery journey.

WEMWBS (Warwick-Edinburgh Scale): A 14-item scale measuring mental wellbeing, widely used in UK health services to monitor positive mental health.

Recovery Star: A collaborative outcomes tool that supports and measures change in key areas of life and recovery.

🛡️ Safeguarding Vulnerable Adults - Quick Tips

  • Recognize signs: Unexplained injuries, poor hygiene, sudden behavior changes, withdrawal, or financial problems
  • Report concerns: Contact local Adult Social Services or call 999 in emergencies
  • Never ignore: If something feels wrong, report it - you could save a life
  • Local safeguarding: Bristol: 0117 922 2700 (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm) or 01454 615 165 (out of hours)
  • National helpline: Action on Elder Abuse 0808 808 8141

1. Recognition & Seeking Help

Acknowledge difficulties → Contact GP or self-refer to IAPT → Crisis support if needed

2. Assessment & Support Planning

Professional assessment → Personalized care plan → Medication and/or therapy options

3. Active Treatment & Management

Attend appointments → Practice self-care strategies → Monitor progress with tools like WEMWBS

4. Recovery & Maintenance

Build resilience → Develop coping strategies → Engage in community activities → Maintain wellbeing

5. Thriving & Prevention

Live meaningfully → Support others → Regular self-monitoring → Early intervention if needed
